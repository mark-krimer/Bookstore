The Master and Margarita

The Master and Margarita is a novel by Mikhail Afanasyevich Bulgakov, work on which began in the late 1920s and continued until the writer's death. The novel refers to unfinished works; After the death of her husband, the writer's widow, Elena Sergeevna, carried out editing and bringing together the draft notes.
